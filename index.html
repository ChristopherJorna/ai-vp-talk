Visual AI

AI in the pipeline.

Unreal stays the render engine. AI fills the gaps.
Example: Riyadh skyline building.

?

Problem

Needed this exact building in Unreal. No 3D mesh anywhere online to kitbash.

1

Nanobana pro

Generate hi-res image of the building on a black background.

2

Meshy.ai → 3D mesh

Image-to-mesh + texture in the same tool.

3

Mask the emissives

Too dark in Unreal. Keep only the blue parts emissive (Photoshop/GIMP).

4

Emissive → Unreal

Apply the masked texture as emissive and plug into the skyline.

✓

Result

Not close-up hero material. But in the skyline at night: it holds up.
